Established in 1928 our institution is one of the esteemed educational institution located in the state of Karnataka. The four sightedness of the honorable Raja of Mysore his Excellency Nalwadi Krishna Raja wadiyar provided the foundation for education of the girl child. This resulted in the establishment of maharani's girls High school. Very soon realizing the empowerment of teacher education, especially women teachers, our institution Maharani's Women's Teacher Training Institute was established in 1928.
