About Us

GOVERNMENT MAHARANI'S WOMEN'S TEACHER TRAINING INSTITUTE - GMWTTI was established In 1917. It is approved by NCTE (National Council for Teacher Education) & affiliated by University of Mysore. The campus of the GOVERNMENT MAHARANI'S WOMEN'S TEACHER TRAINING INSTITUTE - GMWTTI is located in Mysore, Karnataka. The Institute can be reached either by rail or by road. The mission of the GOVERNMENT MAHARANI'S WOMEN'S TEACHER TRAINING INSTITUTE - GMWTTI is to contribute to nation and society at the highest international levels of excellence and to stand on the same scaffold with other universities of eminence.
